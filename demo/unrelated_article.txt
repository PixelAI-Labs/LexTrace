A small balcony garden can thrive with a few simple choices. Start with herbs that tolerate shallow soil, like basil, thyme, and chives. Water early in the day and use mulch to keep moisture from evaporating quickly.

If you only have room for one container, choose a dwarf tomato or a compact pepper variety. Rotate the pot every few days so the plant receives even sunlight, and feed it with a balanced fertilizer once a month.

Gardening is less about perfect schedules and more about paying attention. Healthy leaves, steady growth, and consistent blooms are signals that the environment is right for your plants.
